  • Contract Analysis - Identify unfavorable terms, hidden costs, renewal obligations
  • Supplier Risk - Assess financial stability, concentration risk, geopolitical exposure
  • Negotiation Strategy - Identify leverage points, benchmark against market rates, find alternatives
  • Cost Optimization - Model consolidation opportunities, renegotiation windows, alternative sourcing
